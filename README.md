# ResolveX – Smart Complaint Management System

## 📌 Project Overview
**ResolveX** is a web-based **Smart Complaint Management System** developed to simplify and digitize complaint handling and administrative management processes in residential societies, educational institutions, and workplaces.

The system provides a centralized platform where users can register complaints, track issue status in real-time, receive updates, and communicate efficiently with administrators. It also helps management teams organize records, assign staff roles, manage announcements, and maintain transparency in operations.

# 🧠 Problem Statement
Traditional complaint management systems often rely on manual registers, phone calls, emails, or informal messaging systems, which can result in:
- Delayed responses
- Poor complaint tracking
- Miscommunication
- Loss of records
- Inefficient management processes

ResolveX addresses these challenges by offering a structured and digital complaint management platform.

---

# 💡 Proposed Solution
ResolveX provides a centralized digital platform that allows users to:
- Submit complaints regarding maintenance, security, electricity, or other services
- Monitor complaint progress
- Receive updates in real time

Administrators can:
- Manage complaints efficiently
- Assign tasks to staff members
- Monitor activities
- Maintain proper records and documentation
